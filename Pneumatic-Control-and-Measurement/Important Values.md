# Electric Regulators
## P31PA92AD2VD1A
### Electrical Characteristics
- Power in: 24V DC, 1.1W max
- Control signal: 0-10V DC, with Ri=100Kohm
- Output signal: Digital PNP

### Pneumatic Characteristics
- Port specs: 1/4"F NPT for I/O/E
- Regulating pressure range: 0-145 PSI
- Min inlet pressure: set pressure + 7.25 PSI
- Max inlet pressure: 152 PSI

## ITV2050-04N3S4-X27
### Electrical Characteristics
- Power in: 24V DC, 2.9W max
- Control signal: 4-20mA DC (sink type), with Ri<250ohm
- Output signal: 4-20mA DC (sink type) max load impedance 250 ohm

### Pneumatic Characteristics
- Port specs: 3/8"F NPT for I/O, 1/4"F NPT for E, but sits on manifold with 1/4"F NPT for I and 3/8"F NPT for O
- Regulating pressure range: 0.725-130 PSI
- Min inlet pressure: set pressure + 14.5 PSI
- Max inlet pressure: 145 PSI
